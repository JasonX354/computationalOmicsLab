INTRODUCTION TO POKEMONS

Pokemon is a wholesome game for the family as children capture wild animals to fight in arenas in-game. The game teaches valuable life lessons to the kids, taking over the role of parent. Founded by Nintendo, Pokemon is a multi-billionaire franchise that contains 100+ creatures for children to take care of. However, only 151 pokemon will be featured in this webpage. Personally, I would rate the game a solid 151/10, one point for each pokemon. Click "AllPokemon" To view a table of all 151 pokemon, or hover over "Types" to sort by type. Additionally, you can press "Top 10" to view my own personal top 10 pokemon.
